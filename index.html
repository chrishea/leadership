Leadership Cloudcroft aims to identify and prepare community leaders to address our mountain village's specific challenges. Through monthly classes taught by expert speakers, site visits, and hands-on projects, we connect diverse residents who want to make a difference.

The Challenge

As a small mountain village, Cloudcroft depends on volunteers from the community to lead its local institutions: the government, school board, library, fire department, and numerous committees that help shape its public services. Despite these volunteers' critical role, there is no formal effort to identify, recruit, or train these leaders. Most take office with no background or experience in their new jobs. That lack of preparation can lead to oversights, mistakes, and missed opportunities.

The Goals

Our program builds relationships between local and seasonal neighbors, increases understanding of Cloudcroft's needs, and creates a growing network of informed leaders ready to take action for our community's future.
